Charly & Johayron Piña Colada is a tropical cocktail that originated in Puerto Rico. It is made with fresh pineapple juice, coconut cream, and white rum. The cocktail is typically garnished with a pineapple wedge and a maraschino cherry, adding a pop of color to the drink.

How to Make Charly & Johayron Piña Colada

To make Charly & Johayron Piña Colada, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 2 oz white rum
  • 4 oz pineapple juice
  • 2 oz coconut cream
  • 1 cup ice
  • Pineapple wedge and maraschino cherry, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, combine the rum, pineapple juice, coconut cream, and ice.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour into a glass and garnish with a pineapple wedge and maraschino cherry.
  4. Enjoy!
