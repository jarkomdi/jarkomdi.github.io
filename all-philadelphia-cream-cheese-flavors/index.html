All Philadelphia Cream Cheese Flavors: A Brief History

Philadelphia Cream Cheese has a long and storied history that dates back to the late 19th century. It was first created by a dairy farmer named William Lawrence in New York. Lawrence's unique process for making cream cheese resulted in a product that was smoother and creamier than any other on the market. The Philadelphia Cream Cheese brand was born in 1880 when Lawrence partnered with the Phenix Cheese Company in Philadelphia. Since then, Philadelphia Cream Cheese has become a household name and a staple in kitchens across America.
