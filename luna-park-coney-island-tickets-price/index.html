Luna Park Coney Island Tickets Price and Options

Luna Park Coney Island offers a variety of ticket options to suit different budgets and preferences. The prices vary depending on factors such as the age of the visitor, the time of year, and the type of ticket you choose.

One of the most popular options is the Luna Pass, which provides unlimited access to all the rides at Luna Park for a specified period of time. The prices for the Luna Pass range from $69 for a one-day pass to $159 for a seven-day pass. This is a great option if you're planning to spend a full day or more at the park and want to experience as many rides as possible.

If you're only planning to visit Luna Park for a few hours or want to try out a few rides without committing to a full day, you can purchase individual ride tickets. These tickets are priced at $10 each and can be used for any ride in the park. Keep in mind that some of the more popular rides may require multiple tickets, so it's a good idea to check the ride requirements before purchasing.

Another option is the Scream Pass, which allows you to skip the lines for certain rides and attractions. The prices for the Scream Pass range from $25 for a one-day pass to $60 for a seven-day pass. This is a great option if you want to make the most of your time at Luna Park and avoid long wait times.
