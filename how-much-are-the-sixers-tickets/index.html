The History and Myth of Sixers Tickets

The Philadelphia 76ers, also known as the Sixers, have a rich history in the NBA. The team was founded in 1946 as the Syracuse Nationals and later moved to Philadelphia in 1963. This storied history has contributed to the myth and allure of Sixers tickets, making them highly sought after.
