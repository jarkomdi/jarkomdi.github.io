What is rip rap?

Rip rap, also known as shot rock or rock armor, is a type of rock or stone that is used to protect shorelines, riverbanks, and other areas from erosion. It is typically installed in areas where water is present, such as along the edges of lakes, rivers, and streams.

Rip rap is made up of large, angular rocks that are tightly packed together. This design allows the rocks to absorb the impact of waves or flowing water, preventing erosion and protecting the underlying soil. Rip rap can also provide habitat for aquatic plants and animals, making it an important tool for ecological restoration.
