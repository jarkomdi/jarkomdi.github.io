The Pain Points of Israel vs Palestina

The conflict between Israel and Palestine has resulted in immense suffering for both sides. Both Israelis and Palestinians have experienced loss of life, displacement, and economic hardship. It is important to acknowledge that there is no easy answer to the question of who is right in the Israel-Palestine conflict. Both sides have legitimate grievances and claims to the land.
