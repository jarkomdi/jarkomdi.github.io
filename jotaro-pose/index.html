What is Jotaro Pose?

The Jotaro pose is a signature gesture of the character Jotaro Kujo from the anime and manga series "JoJo's Bizarre Adventure". It involves crossing your arms in an "X" shape in front of your chest, with your hands clenched into fists and your thumbs pointing outward. This pose is often accompanied by a serious expression and a determined stare. The Jotaro pose is a symbol of power, confidence, and the unwavering resolve of the character.

The Jotaro pose originated in the third arc of the "JoJo's Bizarre Adventure" series, titled "Stardust Crusaders". Jotaro Kujo, the protagonist of this arc, is known for his stoic demeanor and powerful Stand abilities. The pose became an iconic symbol of Jotaro's character, representing his indomitable will and determination to protect his friends and defeat his enemies. Over the years, the Jotaro pose has become a beloved gesture among fans of the series, and is often imitated and referenced in other media.
