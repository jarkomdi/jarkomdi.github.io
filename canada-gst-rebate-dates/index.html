What is Canada GST Rebate?

The Canada GST rebate is a refundable tax credit designed to help lower-income individuals and families offset the Goods and Services Tax (GST) they pay on essential goods and services. The rebate amount varies based on factors such as family size and income level. It is important to note that the GST rebate is separate from the Canada Child Benefit, which provides additional financial support for families with children.

The calculation of the Canada GST rebate is based on the GST you have paid on essential goods and services. The amount of the rebate is determined by your income level and family size.
