What is Independence Day 2024 Israel?

Independence Day 2024 Israel, also known as Yom Ha'atzmaut, commemorates the declaration of the establishment of the State of Israel in 1948. This day is a time for Israelis to reflect on their history, honor the sacrifices made by their ancestors, and express their gratitude for the freedom and independence they enjoy today.
