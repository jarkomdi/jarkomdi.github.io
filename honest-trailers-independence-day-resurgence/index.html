Honest Trailers Independence Day Resurgence

Released in 2016, "Independence Day: Resurgence" is a sequel to the highly popular movie "Independence Day" that was released in 1996. The movie is set in a world where Earth has recovered from the devastating alien attack that took place 20 years ago. However, the aliens return with a vengeance, and it is up to a group of brave humans to save the planet once again.

The movie follows the lives of familiar characters from the first film, including President Whitmore, played by Bill Pullman, and David Levinson, played by Jeff Goldblum. It also introduces new characters, such as Jake Morrison, played by Liam Hemsworth, and Dylan Hiller, played by Jessie T. Usher. The film is filled with thrilling action sequences, stunning visual effects, and a captivating storyline that keeps viewers on the edge of their seats.
