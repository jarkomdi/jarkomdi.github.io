What is CTE Head Injury in Football Players?

CTE (chronic traumatic encephalopathy) is a degenerative brain disease that is commonly found in individuals who have experienced repeated head injuries, such as concussions. In football players, the risk of CTE is particularly high due to the nature of the sport, which involves frequent collisions and blows to the head. CTE is characterized by the accumulation of an abnormal protein called tau in the brain, which leads to the progressive degeneration of brain tissue.
