The colonists felt that their rights and freedoms were being violated, and they wanted to break free from British rule.

The target of the declaration of independence summary was the British government and King George III. The colonists wanted to make it clear that they no longer recognized the authority of the British government and that they were declaring themselves to be an independent nation. They outlined their reasons for seeking independence and asserted their right to self-governance.
