Next, prepare the filling by beating cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth and creamy. Beat in eggs one at a time, making sure to scrape down the sides of the bowl as needed. Pour the filling into the prepared crust and smooth the top with a spatula.

Bake the cheesecake in a preheated oven for about an hour, or until the center is set and the top is lightly golden. Remove the cheesecake from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.

Meanwhile, prepare the sour cream topping by mixing together sour cream, sugar, and vanilla extract. Spread the topping evenly over the cooled cheesecake and return it to the oven for an additional 10 minutes.

Once the cheesecake has cooled completely, refrigerate it for at least 4 hours, or overnight, before serving. This will allow the flavors to meld together and the cheesecake to set.
