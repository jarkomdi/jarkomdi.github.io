What is Israel vs Palestine Explained?

Israel vs Palestine Explained refers to the ongoing conflict between the State of Israel and the Palestinian people. The core issues revolve around land ownership, borders, security, and the right to self-determination. The conflict has deep historical, religious, and political roots, and has been marked by violence, negotiations, and failed peace agreements.

History and Myth of Israel vs Palestine Explained

The history of the Israel vs Palestine conflict dates back to the late 19th century, with the rise of Zionism and the movement for Jewish self-determination. The establishment of the State of Israel in 1948 led to the displacement of hundreds of thousands of Palestinians, creating a refugee crisis that persists to this day.

Both Israelis and Palestinians have deep historical and religious connections to the land, which has contributed to the intensity and intractability of the conflict. The narratives and myths surrounding the conflict often shape public opinion and influence political decisions.
