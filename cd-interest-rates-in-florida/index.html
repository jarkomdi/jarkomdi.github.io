What are CD Interest Rates in Florida?

CD stands for Certificate of Deposit. It is a type of financial product offered by banks and credit unions that allows you to deposit a certain amount of money for a fixed period of time, typically ranging from a few months to several years. In return for depositing your money, the bank or credit union will pay you a fixed interest rate on your investment. CD interest rates in Florida can vary depending on the financial institution and the length of the CD term.
