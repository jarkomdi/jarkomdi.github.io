Recommendations for Tom Cruise Films on Netflix

If you're new to the world of Tom Cruise films or simply looking for a movie to watch, here are some recommendations to get you started:

1. Top Gun (1986)

This classic film follows the story of a young naval aviator, played by Cruise, as he trains at the elite Fighter Weapons School. With its iconic soundtrack and thrilling flight sequences, Top Gun is a must-watch for any fan of action movies.

2. Mission: Impossible - Fallout (2018)

In the latest installment of the Mission: Impossible series, Cruise reprises his role as Ethan Hunt. This action-packed film takes viewers on a wild ride as Hunt and his team race against time to prevent a global catastrophe.

3. Jerry Maguire (1996)

In this romantic comedy-drama, Cruise plays a sports agent who has a moral epiphany and decides to start his own agency. Jerry Maguire is a heartwarming film that showcases Cruise's versatility as an actor.

4. Rain Man (1988)

Cruise stars alongside Dustin Hoffman in this critically acclaimed drama. The film tells the story of two brothers who embark on a cross-country journey that changes their lives forever.
