Lorraine Warren Conjuring: Explained

Lorraine Warren conjuring refers to the paranormal investigations and experiences of Lorraine and her late husband, Ed Warren. Their work inspired the successful Conjuring film franchise, which includes movies like The Conjuring, The Conjuring 2, and Annabelle.

Lorraine Warren's journey into the world of conjuring began when she met Ed Warren, a self-taught demonologist.
