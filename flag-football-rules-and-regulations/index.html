Common questions about flag football rules and regulations

Q: Can you tackle in flag football?

A: No, tackling is not allowed in flag football. Instead of tackling, defenders must remove a player's flag to stop the play.

Q: How many players are on a flag football team?

A: A flag football team typically consists of 7 to 8 players on the field at a time.

Q: Are there any restrictions on passing in flag football?

A: In flag football, all players are eligible to receive a pass, including linemen.

Q: What happens if a flag falls off during a play?

A: If a flag falls off during a play, the player is considered down at the spot where the flag was removed.
