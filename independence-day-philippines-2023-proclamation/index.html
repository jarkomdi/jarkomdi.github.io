The History and Myth of Independence Day Philippines 2023 Proclamation

The history of the proclamation of Independence Day in the Philippines is intertwined with the struggle for freedom from Spanish colonial rule. On June 12, 1898, General Emilio Aguinaldo, the leader of the revolutionary forces, read the Act of the Declaration of Independence in Kawit, Cavite, proclaiming the country's independence from Spain. However, it is important to note that the Philippines did not immediately gain full independence after the proclamation. The country went through several years of colonization and occupation before finally achieving true independence.
